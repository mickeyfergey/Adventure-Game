Welcome to the super duper awesome adventure game!
   
Your goal is to obtain freedom from your family and play Xbox with your best friend, Bob!
The way to escape is simple! Find items around the house that unlock other rooms in the house.
But it gets more complicated from there! You will be scored on how many items you find!
